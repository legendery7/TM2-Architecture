# ADR-003: Reputation milestones as progression spine

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Players need a comprehensible journey from temporary capital rent to city-scale systems.

## Decision

Use landmark buildings and reputation thresholds (HQ → town hall → city) as the progression spine. Unlocks for claims, menus, and warehouse follow milestones.

## Consequences

Clear UX story. Demolition and reputation rollback rules must be explicit.
